About

The Soil Association is the only UK charity that works across the spectrum of human health, the environment and animal welfare. Its organic certification is widely regarded as the gold standard for all organic products and is very difficult to achieve. Every product in the Thistle Fields household and laundry range is certified organic with The Soil Association. This means that you can be assured that Thistle Fields products are made from organically farmed ingredients which are not genetically modified (GM) using no herbicides or synthetic fertilisers.
All Thistle Fields products are free from harsh chemicals, nano particles, parabens, synthetic dyes and artificial fragrances.

Our Products are Organic

Many products can call themselves organic but that does not mean that they are 100% organic. In fact, very few products are 100% as they usually contain water and often non-organic ingredients such as synthetic fragrance, which is a common skin and respiratory irritant.
At Thistle Fields all our products are certified by UK certifying body, The Soil Association, which means that our workshop is regularly inspected, all our ingredients are approved by them as well as what we put on our labels and in our marketing material. You will find that other products may be certified by another body such as Ecocert who do not have such stringent conditions.

Ingredients

Each product in the Thistle Fields range is certified organic by The Soil Association and contains more than 70% certified organic ingredients. Other ingredients are sourced from a restricted natural palette that are all approved by The Soil Association.
