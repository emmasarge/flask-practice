This means that you can be assured that Thistle Fields products are made from organically farmed ingredients which are not genetically modified (GM) using no herbicides or synthetic fertilisers.
All Thistle Fields products are free from harsh chemicals, nano particles, parabens, synthetic dyes and artificial fragrances.
